Dr Fatumina Said Abukar

Co-founder/ CEO

Dr Fatumina is an award-winning Doctoral graduand from UCL who has expertise in Regenerative Medicine Bioprocessing for heart cell therapy. She was shortlisted for several prestigious awards including the WISE Rising Star Award presented by the Princess Royal, she is an entrepreneur with her successful henna-art business 'hennapopp', science communicator, and public speaker. Fatumina is an advocate for increasing the female talent pipeline within science and engineering and was also shortlisted for New Outstanding Stem Inspiration Award 2017 held at the House of Parliament. However unconventional, she has used the power of social media with her Instagram channel ‘fatuminamusic’ (which has ~30,000 followers) to reach out to a wider audience and she tries to be a role model for other women who believe that engineering and being creative are mutually exclusive.

Lily Uznadze

Co-founder/ Editor-In-Chief

Lili is a recent master’s graduate from UCL with a degree in Management. Throughout her university years, she helped organise a variety of charity fundraising events, such as London Universities Modest Fashion Show, TEDx and CapGemini Challenge. She is passionate about the application of technology and innovation in order to help companies grow and improve their performance.

Melissa Mead

Co-founder/ Director of Growth

Melissa has been a Project Manager for UCL Innovation and Enterprise since early 2018. Before this she was in charge of running outreach programmes for young carers and students under-represented in Higher Education at UCL and the University of Oxford. She has developed an online toolkit for supporting young carers in schools, and is passionate about ensuring disadvantaged students can apply to (and succeed at) top-tier universities. In a few months she'll finish her degree in English at Birkbeck, after which she'll focus on fulfilling her dream of becoming an extra in EastEnders.
