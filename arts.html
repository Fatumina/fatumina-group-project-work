Women Arts

Arts & Music

Girls Rock

Girls Rock London is a music project for girls and women in London. The aims of the project are to empower girls and women to write and perform music and to build self-confidence. The programme runs a mixture of musical/technical tuition and workshops, which together help to build participants’ self-esteem.

Funny Women

Funny Women is UK’s leading female comedy community. Founded in 2002 to help women find their voice through performing, writing and using humour in business and everyday life. They run comedy workshops, weekend events and conferences in London, Manchester, Brighton and Edinburgh on stand up, comedy writing, improvisation and sketch and character.

Women's Social Meetups in London

Would you like to meet more gal pals with similar hobbies? Then you might be interested in Women's Social Meetups in London. Here you can find an array of women-only meetup groups according to your interests and location.

Soul Stripped Sessions

Stripped Soul Sessions is a unique intimate live music experience. They feature the finest hand-picked female soulful singer-songwriters. The monthly events bring impressive female artists, performing original songs and covering songs by female soul artists you know & love.

Cooking with Mama

Cooking with Mama is a social enterprise and cookery school in London. It aims to create meaningful culinary experiences for participants to savour different cultures through its food while learning traditional recipes from our mamas.

Black British Female Artist (BBFA) Collective

This collective was born out of the lack of representation of Black British Female Artists and to provide a platform for emerging artists to showcase their works and let the art world know that they are here!

Rocking Ur Teens

Rocking Ur Teen aims to equip young people with the skills that leaders of the future will need, including entrepreneurial spirit, agility & flexibility, self esteem and good mental health. They also happen to carry out Teen Girls Empowerment Conferences focusing on 13-14 year old females.

Muslim Sisterhood

Muslim Sisterhood is a collective exploring Muslim womanhood through a photo series dedicated to the representation of everyday young Muslim women around London. The collective wants to stamp out gentrification and give muslim women the recognition they deserve.
