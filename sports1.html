Sports & Fitness

Sports & Fitness

Roller Derby

Learn to skate in a fun, safe, and friendly environment, or go and watch games to cheer on some of the fiercest females in sport! London Roller Girls are a great place to start and have a new intake in January. London Rockin' Rollers are another team who play in Tottenham. Closer to Croydon? Try Croydon Roller Derby.

Go Mammoth

From netball, to dodgeball, to volleyball to rounders - there are loads of sports leagues available through Go Mammoth. There's a tonne of mixed opportunities, but lots just for women too - all across London! For most of them you can enter your own team, or join one made up of new people.

Adidas Women's Studio

The Adidas Women's Studio run a heap of fitness classes for women in their Brick Lane studio. You can use the space to catch up, drink a smoothie, or sweat with some pals.

Muslim Women in Sport Foundation

With the aim of getting more Muslim women into sport, they host a lot of sport events and meet-ups around London (and the UK!). Check out their events page for the calendar of events!

Women's Boxing Classes

There are a lot of women's boxing classes in London. Miguel's is based near Brixton Station, and has great reviews. There are beginner classes, classes for just working out, and classes for if you want to get competitive.

This Girl Can: Lambeth

If you're 16-25 and based near Lambeth, This Girl Can have an amazing range of sports opportunities. Zumba, Street Dance, Football, Cycling and Circuit Classes are just some of the activities on offer.

London FA: Women & Girls Football

Playing and coaching opportunities are listed for women and girls across London. Currently the most popular sport for females in the UK!

London Women's Rugby

With the rugby world cup fast approaching, there's still time to give this sport a 'try'. This page lists many of the clubs operating across Greater London.
