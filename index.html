We help the women leaders of tomorrow

About Company Page


Gals Guide was funded by powerful and inspirational women who want to actively help other women to succeed in areas where their talent is not nurtured.


Gals Guide is a community of passionate women who want to change the face of music and arts, tech and sports. We are here to provide support to the women of tomorrow and take action through community meetings, events and shared experiences. We are here to stand for the women whose voices are silenced.

Our Values


MISSION: We are here to support women who want to become change makers of the future


VISION: We aim to be an inclusive platform pushing the concept of diversity forward regardless of age, ethnicity, gender, religion, ability, experience, class, size, and more.
